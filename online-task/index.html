Directions

1. A 4x4 grid will appear on the left side of the screen
2. Numbers 1-16 will be randomly placed in the grid
3. A timer will count down from 10 seconds
4. During that time, memorize the order of the numbers
5. When the timer reaches 0, the numbers will dissapear
6. Click the squares in the order of the previous numbers
7. Finish in the shortest time with as few errors as possible!
8. Click the "Okay" button to start the timer and the game

Your performance on this task will be collected for a research study.
No personal identifying information will be collected.
By clicking "Okay" you are acknowledging and consenting to the previous conditions.
