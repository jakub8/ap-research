Directions

1. A 4x4 grid will appear on the left of the screen
2. Numbers 1-16 will be randomly placed in the grid
3. A timer will count down from 10 seconds
4. During that time, memorize the order of the numbers
5. When the timer reaches 0, the numbers will dissapear
6. Click the squares in the order of the previous numbers
   -correctly clicked squares will turn green and the number will reveal
   -incorrectly clicked squares will turn red briefly
7. Reveal all squares as quick and error-free as possible
   -errors and a timer will be shown throughout
8. Click the "Okay" button to start the timer and the game
